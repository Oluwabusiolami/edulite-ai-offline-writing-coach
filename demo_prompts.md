# EduLite AI Demo Prompts

These are sample prompts for testing EduLite AI as an offline English writing coach for African secondary schools.

## Demo 1: Essay Feedback

A Year 10 student has written this paragraph:

"I was walking inside the dark house. I was afraid. I heard something. I ran away."

Give simple feedback. Explain how the student can create more suspense. Rewrite the paragraph in a better way while keeping it simple.

## Demo 2: IGCSE ESL Email Practice

Help a beginner English learner write a 150-word informal email to a friend about preparing for school examinations. Use simple vocabulary, clear paragraphs and a friendly tone.

## Demo 3: Vocabulary Builder

Explain the word "resilient" for a secondary school student.

Include:

* pronunciation
* part of speech
* simple meaning
* two example sentences
* one practice question

## Demo 4: Teacher Feedback Mode

A student wrote:

"My best day was when we went to beach. I enjoy with my friends and we eat rice. It was very fun and I will never forgot it."

Give teacher-style feedback. Identify the grammar mistakes. Rewrite the paragraph correctly.

## Demo 5: Creative Writing Helper

A student wants to write a story titled "The Missing Bag".

Give:

* a story plan
* three possible characters
* a suspenseful beginning
* five strong vocabulary words the student can use
